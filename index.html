About Kate

With over a decade of experience in watercolor and gouache painting, I have dedicated my artistic journey to exploring the delicate balance between control and spontaneity that these mediums offer.

My work draws inspiration from the natural world, urban landscapes, and the subtle emotions that color our daily experiences. Each piece is an exploration of light, transparency, and the unique properties that make watercolor and gouache such captivating mediums.

I believe that art should speak to the soul, creating moments of pause and reflection in our busy world. Through my paintings, I aim to capture not just what we see, but what we feel.
